Photo Studio

The studio is used regularly for fashion editorial work, commercials and music videos.

We believe that high-end fashion photography shouldn’t be exclusive to high-end fashion models

The photography studio is located on the 2nd level of the building, which has fantastic natural light from the skylight at the north end of the studio. The studio offers clients the opportunity to have three shoots running at once and access to the rest of the building for location shots. The studio is used regularly for fashion editorial work, commercials and music videos. There is a fully equipped hair and make-up and styling room. There is also an accessible meeting room for castings and client meetings.
